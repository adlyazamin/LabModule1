About UMT

VISION & MISSION

UMT’s Philosophy: The integration of knowledge and practice based on faith in Allah is fundamental to the university’s endeavour in preparing competent human capital for global prosperity.

Vision: A marine-focused university, reputed nationally and respected globally.

Mission: Generating knowledge for the prosperity of the community and world sustainability.

Educational Goals: Producing competent graduates who possess the value of ihsan for society’s well-being and environmental sustainability.

Slogan: Ocean of Discoveries, for Global Sustainability.

Niche Area: Marine Science and Aquatic Resources.
